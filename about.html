Our Story

From a small passion project to your favorite coffee spot.

Born from Passion

Nimna Coffee started with a simple idea: that coffee should be more than just a morning routine—it should be an experience. Founded in 2020, we embarked on a journey to find the perfect beans, master the art of roasting, and create a space where community thrives.

We believe in sustainability and quality. Every bean we brew is ethically sourced, supporting farmers who care about the land as much as we do about the cup. Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a long conversation, we welcome you to the family.
